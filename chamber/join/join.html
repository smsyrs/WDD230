NP Membership

This membership is specifically for Non-Profit Organizations. There is no fees assocaited with the level of membership

Bronze Memberships

The bronze membership is the lowest tier offered by us and therefore the cheapest. This tier does not have access to as many of the benefits assocaited with the higher tiers (Silver and Gold) such as extra trainings, spotlights, and event discounts.

Price Annually: $300

Silver Membership

The silver membership is the most common tier that is offered. Benefits include extra trainings, spotlight, and event discounts (25%).

Price Annually: $400

Gold Membership

This is our top tier and you will be rewarded. We offer an additional 15 trainings a year for members of this tier, an additional spotlight, and half off all events.

Price Annually: $500
