Temples:

The Fort Collins Colorado Temple is located in beautiful Northern Colorado about 50 miles south of the Wyoming capital of Cheyenne. It is also located only a couple miles from Interstate 25 and stands tall over the homes and buildings in Fort Collins. Fort Collins has a beautiful downtown area, which is affectionally called "Old Town", and additionally is home to the Colorado State Univeristy Rams. Old Town is one of the inspirations for Main Street Disney Land. The Fort Collins Temple was dedicated in 2016 after its groundbreaking in 2013 and announcement in 2011. It is also among 2 fully constructed temples in Colorado with the Grand Junction Colorado Temple currently under construction. Find out more about this regal building at ChurchofJesusChrist.org and Our Temple Page
