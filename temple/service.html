Receptions:

We have the ability to accomdate up to 150 guests in our Wedding Reception Hall with folding doors to possibly separate the space. We have a simple catering menu to pick options from such as:

  • Chicken & Rice
  • Steak & Potatoes
  • Pork Chops & Pasta

If that does not interest you, we do have a full kitchen that you and your guests can use. You will also have access to chairs and tables of couple different kinds for optimal customabilty for the cost. Contact us Here.

For Full-Time Temple Missionaries:

We thank you for your willingness to serve a mission and in the temple. We have a couple different options for Temple Missionaries at Temple Inn & Suites. Both of those options have a small kitchenette and living room, the first is a Double Queen room and the second is a Single King room. Some additional features of these rooms are that they have a full walk-in closet and a sleeper sofa that has a queen mattress. For more information contact us here.
