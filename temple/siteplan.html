Site Plan

Site Purpose

This purpose of this site to provide valuable information for the current and future customers of Temple Inn & Suites, a hotel chain that strives to build accommodating provisions near temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This valuable information relates to the area the hotels are near, services provided, and resources to reserve rooms in the hotel for patrons.

Target Audience

The target audience for this web page are members or friends of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Since these hotels are primarily located near Temples, the audience are those who wish to attend the temple in a couple different capacities such as worker, patrons, and their families. The some of the services within the hotel are geared towards those who will be attending the temple or particpating in the following events: sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.
